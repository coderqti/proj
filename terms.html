Terms and Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to TopWorld Movers. This document outlines the terms of service and privacy policy that govern your use of our moving and relocation services. By engaging our services, you agree to these terms and our data practices as described in this policy.

2. Terms of Service

2.1 Service Agreement

  • TopWorld Movers agrees to provide moving services as outlined in the formal quote provided to the client
  • All quotes are valid for 30 days from the date of issuance
  • Final charges may vary based on actual time, materials, and additional services required

2.2 Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information about items to be moved
  • Ensure all items are properly packed and ready for transport
  • Disclose any fragile, valuable, or dangerous items
  • Obtain necessary parking permits or building approvals
  • Be present or have a representative available during the move

2.3 Liability and Insurance

  • TopWorld Movers maintains comprehensive insurance coverage
  • Standard liability coverage is included in all moves
  • Additional insurance options are available for purchase
  • Claims must be filed within 7 days of service completion

We are not liable for:

  • Pre-existing damage
  • Items packed by the client
  • Force majeure events

2.4 Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before scheduled move
  • 50% charge for cancellations within 24-48 hours
  • Full charge for cancellations less than 24 hours before scheduled move
