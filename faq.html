Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do TopWorld Movers serve?

TopWorld Movers provides comprehensive moving services throughout Kenya, with a focus on Nairobi and major cities. We also offer international moving services to destinations worldwide, specializing in both residential and commercial relocations.

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for local moves and 4-6 weeks for international moves. However, we understand that sometimes moves happen unexpectedly, and we'll do our best to accommodate your timeline.

What packing services do you offer?

We offer full packing services, including providing all necessary materials. Our professional team can pack your entire home or office, or we can pack specific items upon request. We also offer unpacking services at your destination.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Yes, TopWorld Movers provides basic insurance coverage for all moves. We also offer additional comprehensive insurance options for valuable items. Our team will explain the coverage details during the quote process.

How do you handle fragile or valuable items?

We take extra precautions with fragile and valuable items, using specialized packing materials and techniques. Our movers are trained in handling delicate items, and we can provide custom crating for extremely valuable or sensitive items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including M-PESA, bank transfers, and major credit cards. For international moves, we typically require a deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due before delivery.

Do you provide storage solutions?

Yes, we offer both short-term and long-term storage solutions in our secure, climate-controlled facilities. This is particularly useful if there's a gap between your move-out and move-in dates.

What should I do to prepare for my move?

We recommend decluttering before your move, securing important documents, and preparing an essentials box. Our team will provide a detailed moving checklist upon booking to help you prepare effectively.

How do you handle international moves?

For international moves, we handle all aspects including packing, documentation, customs clearance, and delivery. We have established networks worldwide and can provide door-to-door service, ensuring a smooth international relocation.

What sets TopWorld Movers apart from other moving companies?

TopWorld Movers combines years of experience with a commitment to excellence. We offer customized moving solutions, transparent pricing, highly trained staff, and a track record of successful moves. Our customer-centric approach and attention to detail ensure a stress-free moving experience.

Why Choose Us?

We offer customized solutions tailored to meet your unique moving needs. Trust us to handle your belongings with care and professionalism.
